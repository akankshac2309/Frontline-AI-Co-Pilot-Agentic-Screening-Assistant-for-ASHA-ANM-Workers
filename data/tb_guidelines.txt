TB Screening Guidelines (Simplified)

Rule 1:
If patient has cough for more than 14 days → Suspected TB case.

Rule 2:
If cough >14 days + weight loss → High suspicion TB.

Rule 3:
If cough >14 days + fever + night sweats → High suspicion TB.

Action:
Recommend sputum test at nearest health center.

Escalation:
Refer the patient to ANM or doctor for further evaluation.

Important:
This system does NOT diagnose TB.
It only suggests screening actions based on guidelines.
